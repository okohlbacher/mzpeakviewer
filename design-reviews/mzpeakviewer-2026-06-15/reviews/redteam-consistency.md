# Red-team review — Consistency & Accessibility

Auditor: Consistency & A11y. Target: `handoff/` (UNTRUSTED). Ground truth: repo
`packages/ui-kit/src/styles/tokens/*.css`, `app/src/App.tsx`. Contrast ratios computed
with the WCAG 2.1 relative-luminance formula from the actual token hex values.

## Clean categories

- **Primary-accent contrast passes**: white on `--blue-600 #3b54da` = 6.06:1, white on `--blue-700` segmented-active = 7.78:1, blue-600 text on white = 6.06:1, `--red-600` on `--red-50` = 5.91:1, `--warning #8a6d00` on white/amber-bg = 4.9/4.6:1 — all AA. The dark-stage **primary** readout `#e7edf2` (15.9:1) and legend `#9aa4ad` (7.4:1) pass. So the accent/active states are sound; failures are confined to the success-green and the gray-400 muted tier and the gray-500 dark-stage hint tier.
- **Type tokens match the repo**: IBM Plex Sans/Mono via `--font-sans`/`--font-mono`, `tabular-nums` for numerics — consistent with `packages/ui-kit/.../typography.css`.
- **Colormap names** (viridis/inferno/gray) and **R/G/B channel tokens** (`#e53935/#43a047/#1e88e5`) in the DS match the repo and brief exactly.

---

### Summary (top findings)

1. **Blocker — broken nav fidelity:** the wireframe sidebar deletes the shipped `Overlay` MSI tab and mis-states optical gating, while the doc claims it "retains" the real capability-gated nav (`wf-screens-1.js:144-152` vs `app/src/App.tsx:354-359`). It can't be built as described.
2. **Blocker — a11y claims contradict the artifact:** virtually every control is a non-focusable `<span>`/`<div onclick>` with no role/tabindex/key handling, yet the handoff advertises role=button, real tablist + roving tabindex, rows-as-buttons, and keyboard pixel-picking. The exported wireframe demonstrates the opposite of its own accessibility annotations.
3. **Major — three AA contrast failures, all on load-bearing UI:** success-green `#2e9e5b` small text (3.4:1 — including the tag literally labelled "A11y"), the gray-400 muted tier `#9aa4ad` used for placeholders/captions/axis labels (2.5:1), and gray-500 hints on the dark `#0e1216` stage (4.0:1). The accent/active blues are fine; the muted and success tiers are not.
4. **Major — token & data self-contradictions:** the DS `colors.css` header credits a "Material blue (#1565c0)" accent that exists nowhere (real accent `#3b54da`); the imaging file is "Orbitrap" on Summary but a QTOF-class card on Start; one m/z value appears at three precisions; and a 10-colour TMT palette plus inline literal hexes violate the doc's own "No new color tokens" pledge.
5. **Major — no responsive story:** the product mock is a fixed 1160px frame merely transform-scaled to fit; the only media queries live in the handoff doc's own chrome, leaving WCAG 1.4.10 Reflow unaddressed for a brief that flags desktop-first density as a concern.
6. **Minor/Info:** 4-step stepper vs 2-numbered nav use the same numeral motif for different things; 26–30px controls graze the 24px AA target-size floor; the DS ships a render-blocking Google-Fonts `@import` the app deliberately removed.
